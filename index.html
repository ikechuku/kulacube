About Us

Automating, Digitising and reducing paper wastage (saving the trees) one school at a time.

Teacher

Create & Share notes, create & mark assignments, create continuous assessment, student performance review

Parent

Be up to speed with ward grades, work closely with ward teachers to help improve student performance.

Student

View & submit assignment, keep track of individual performance during the school year.
